# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Businesses and individuals in Singapore (and beyond) actively searching for a web developer to hire. They arrive sceptical — they need to feel confident they're not hiring someone inexperienced. Decision-makers in their 20s–40s who browse on desktop, compare multiple agencies, and decide fast. The job: evaluate quality, check pricing, book a call.

## Product Purpose

PrismStatic.web is the client-acquisition engine for a Singapore-based freelance web development agency. It exists to convert visitors into booked discovery calls. Success means a prospect lands on the site, immediately feels the quality, browses work and services without friction, and hits "Book a call" before leaving.

## Brand Personality

Bold, sharp, premium. The site should feel like it was built by someone who takes craft seriously — not a student, not a freelance marketplace profile, not a template. High-confidence energy. Controlled restraint broken by moments of precise animation.

## Anti-references

- Typical AI-generated agency sites: cream/sand backgrounds, small-caps eyebrow labels above every section, gradient hero text, identical icon + heading + text card grids, numbered section markers (01/02/03), hero-metric stat blocks. These are the tells. Avoid every one of them.
- Fiverr/Upwork profile aesthetics: crowded, low-signal, budget-marketplace energy.
- Corporate enterprise sites: stiff, stock-photo heavy, zero personality.

## Design Principles

1. **Restraint breaks loud** — default to stillness; animation earns attention rather than demanding it.
2. **Craft is the pitch** — the site is itself a portfolio piece. Every detail a client notices (cursor, transition, spacing) is an implicit proof of skill.
3. **No emojis, ever** — SVG icons only. Emojis undercut the premium positioning.
4. **Human-coded feel** — avoid every saturated AI pattern (eyebrows, gradient text, cream bg, card grids). Make choices that feel deliberate and specific, not template-derived.
5. **Convert, don't impress** — beauty serves the CTA. Every page should make it easy and obvious to book a call.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as a baseline. No motion for users who prefer reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`). All interactive elements keyboard-navigable. Sufficient contrast for body text against dark backgrounds.
